# Hyderabad Street Food Local Guide

Hyderabad is known for rich, spicy, and flavorful street food.

## Famous Street Foods
- Pani Puri: Extremely spicy and tangy, popular near Charminar.
- Irani Chai: Served with Osmania biscuits, famous at Nimrah Caf√©.
- Haleem: Slow-cooked meat dish, popular during Ramzan in Old City.
- Dosa: Popular South Indian food, common in Ameerpet and Madhapur.
- Shawarma: Popular evening street food in Tolichowki and Mehdipatnam.

## Food Preferences
- People prefer spicy and non-vegetarian food.
- Evening and late-night street food culture is very popular.
- Old City is famous for authentic non-veg dishes.
